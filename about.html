About Us

Specta Hub is a newly established eyewear boutique in Pakistan that offers professional and advanced eye care services. Our motto, "see the world in a new way," is at the core of our business. Since our inception in 2023, we have become a leader in the distribution and sales of premium eyewear for men, women, and children. We take pride in being a futuristic company that has become an authorized heritage within families across the country. Initially, we started as a factory that manufactured frames for export to renowned optical boutiques in other countries. However, over time, Specta Hub has grown exponentially into a full-blown business that provides high-end optical accessories and exceptional services to residents across Pakistan. We are proud to have pioneered the concept of eyewear export within the country, being the first among our competitors to do so. Our mission is to provide top-quality products to our customers at reasonable prices, delivered right to their doorstep.
